AboutUs

At Umang we work with less privileged children education, sanitation & hygiene programs for women, Skill development & employability skills, Mental health awareness. ​

Umang is about bringing Happy changes around us. It is about doing things we can, to improve the lives of the many deserving needy. We inherently believe that no one shuns education voluntarily. It is the circumstances and situations that compel a child to stop being a student and one of the reasons for children giving up on education is that they do not have raw material to continue going to school. We want to provide education to all: no child should be left behind and change the world for good! ​

“We were set up in January 2009 and have grown stronger since then. The great end of education is to discipline rather than to furnish the mind; to train it to the use of its own powers rather than to fill it with the accumulation of others. Education would be much more effective if its purpose was to ensure that by the time they leave, everyone should know how much they do not know, and be imbued with a lifelong desire to know it. The whole purpose of Umang is to ensure that education is given with a purpose of either employability or entrepreneurship. We believe that education gives us knowledge of the world around us and changes it into something better.

Sustainable Development Goals (SDG)

Umang Foundation Trust is well aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals to contribute to a sustainable future by aligning its initiatives with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

Umang Foundation Trust aims to dedicatedly work towards creating a world where every child has the opportunity to reach their full potential. We are committed to addressing the pressing issues of poverty and inequality, particularly among underprivileged children.

Umang Foundation Trust has been providing nutritious food to ZP Schools our learning center children, we aim to alleviate hunger and malnutrition among underprivileged children, contributing to poverty reduction.

Umang Foundation Trust prioritizes the health and well-being of all, particularly among underprivileged communities. We aim to create a society where individuals can access mental health support and move towards awareness and healthy lifestyles.

Umang Foundation Trust aims to work towards a world where every child, regardless of their background, has access to quality education and the opportunity to reach their full potential. Remedial Classes, Creative Workshops, and Educational Activities are empowering them with knowledge and skills.

Women's Health and Sanitation Hygiene Education Program (WHSHEP) of Umang Foundation Trust aims to break the stigma surrounding menstruation and promote menstrual hygiene. By providing sanitary pads and educating girls about menstrual health, we empower them to manage their periods with dignity and confidence.

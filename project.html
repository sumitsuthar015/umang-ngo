Umang Foundation Trust Journey
#BeUmang

Over the years, Umang Foundation Trust has grown with every project we do. We are thankful to our supporters who helped us reach thousands of children and families. From education to mental health, from awareness drives to welfare programs, our vision is to empower communities and spread hope.

Since its inception, Umang Foundation Trust has impacted 10,000+ individuals, connected with 6,000+ schools, and supported 8,000+ students and individuals with education and resources.
